Capture The Flag (CTF)

A cybersecurity challenge to test your skills in problem-solving, cryptography, and ethical hacking.

Event Date

12th November 2025

Time

10:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Event Guidelines

General Rules

  • Each team must consist of exactly two members. Individual participation is not allowed.
  • All teams must register before the start of the competition.
  • Cheating, collusion, or sharing flags/solutions between teams is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Do not publish or share solutions until the event concludes.
  • The decision of the organizers is final.

Technical Rules

  • Participants will be provided with a dedicated computer and a standardized lab environment.
  • Attacks on the CTF infrastructure (e.g., DoS attacks, brute-force) are forbidden.
  • Participants may only use the standard tools provided in the lab.
  • Use of AI tools, automated scanners, or external scripts is strictly prohibited.

Scoring & Submissions

  • Points are awarded for each successful flag submission.
  • In case of a tie, the team that reached the final score first will be ranked higher.
  • Flags must be submitted only via the official CTF platform.
