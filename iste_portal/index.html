About YenSplash '25

YenSplash '25 is the 21st ISTE Karnataka State Level Student Convention, a premier event designed to ignite innovation, foster collaboration, and showcase technical prowess among students. This convention serves as a dynamic platform for aspiring engineers and tech enthusiasts from across the state to compete, learn, and network. Join us for a series of challenging events, workshops, and talks that promise to push the boundaries of your knowledge and creativity.
