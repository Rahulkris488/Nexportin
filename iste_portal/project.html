Project Expo

Showcase your innovation, technical skills, and creativity through live demonstrations and working models.

Event Date

12th November 2025

Time

01:30 PM - 03:30 PM

Event Guidelines

Eligibility & Participation

  • Students from any branch or discipline are eligible.
  • Participants must carry a valid college ID card.
  • Teams must consist of a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members.
  • All selected participants will receive a participation certificate.

Exhibition Setup

  • Each team will be provided a stall/table for their demonstration.
  • One power socket (230V, 50Hz AC) will be available per team.
  • Internet facility must be requested in advance.
  • Flammable fluids or hazardous materials are strictly prohibited.

Judging & Evaluation

  • Projects will be evaluated on innovation, working demonstration, applicability, and presentation quality.
  • Prizes will be awarded to the best projects.
  • The decision of the judges and organizing committee will be final.
